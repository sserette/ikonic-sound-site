About Us

Ikonic Sound strives to bring you to the forefront of today's music industry. Formed by music enthusiasts Troy Manley and Ryan Weber, Ikonic Sound has drawn talent from across the United States and assembled a team of writers that live, breath, and eat music. We are always striving to bring you to the forefront of the music industry. We also want to help upcoming producers get their tunes out for our worldwide audience to enjoy and discover - so send us your UNRELEASED tunes you would like to PREMIERE on our site to IkonicSound@gmail.com! There is surely something for everyone so don't be afraid to wander around the site and figure things out a bit. Don't forget to like us on Facebook and Twitter so you are always up to date on the newest posts and happenings of the music world.
